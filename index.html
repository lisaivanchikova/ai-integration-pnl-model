2.5 Lusin signal-triggered · наш unfair advantage

Internal pilot data (Cyborg HR / Peter Kelly, April 2026):
• Cold outreach без сигнала: 3% reply rate
• Cold outreach с Lusin сигналом: 30% reply rate
→ ×10 lift validated. Переводит Targeted outbound (rank #4 standard) в rank #2-3 по CAC. Снижает effective CAC outbound с $11K до ~$4K.

2.6 Capacity benchmarks

  • Engineer billable utilization realistic: 60-70% · industry avg 68.9%
  • Engineer monthly billable @ 65%: ~95 hrs/mo
  • Integration project: 40-60 hrs typical → ~1.75 implementations/mo per engineer
  • SDR research Lusin-assisted: 5-7 min/touch (vs manual 15-30 min)

2.7 Payment terms + L2 churn

  • Standard: 30% advance / 40% milestone M+1 / 30% net-30 after delivery (M+3)
  • L2 monthly churn anchor: 3% (SMB tier · annual 10-15%)

4.5 So What

Value is built mid-funnel (Paid Pilot filter + early cash) and in the tail (Monthly retainer = 50%+ of LTV). Spend first on referrals, warm network, и Lusin-powered targeted outbound — defer paid ads until Phase 3. Diag→Pilot conversion = highest-leverage point (25-40% — biggest drop).

4.6 Risk caveat

All ratios assume founder closes during Phase 0-1. Hand selling to AE before Paid Pilot is productized → win-rate collapses, CAC rises, economics break. Advance by trigger, never by calendar.

Lusin lift (×10) based on single pilot (Cyborg HR / Peter Kelly, April 2026); validate across 10+ deals before locking as benchmark.
