Core thesis

Accounting firms face a structural pain that incumbents do not solve: cross-system reconciliation between Sage Intacct, QuickBooks Online, Karbon, document portals, and bank/AP/AR ledgers. Karbon Kai (launched June 3, 2026) is workflow-layer. Botkeeper was bookkeeping outsourcing. Bench was consumer-grade and shut down December 2024. The mid-market boutique slot ($25-40K ACV) sits below enterprise consultancies (Distyl, Eudia, Harvey) and above SaaS subscription tools. Referrals dominate this market (~70% of new clients per AICPA PCPS surveys); paid channels are weak.

Sources: AICPA PCPS National MAP Survey 2024; Bench shutdown coverage TechCrunch Dec 27 2024; Karbon Kai launch Accounting Today Jun 3 2026; PitchBook Accounting Roll-up Tracker Q1 2025.
