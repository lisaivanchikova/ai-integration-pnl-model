4.5 So What

Value is built mid-funnel (Paid Pilot filter + early cash) and in the tail (Monthly retainer = 50%+ of LTV arrives after Year 1). Spend first on referrals, warm network, and Lusin-powered targeted outbound — defer paid ads until Phase 3. Diag→Pilot conversion is the highest-leverage point in the funnel (25-40% — biggest drop, see Section 2.3 BOTTLENECK).

4.6 Risk caveat

All ratios assume founder closes during Phase 0-1. Hand selling to AE before Paid Pilot is productized → win-rate collapses, CAC rises, economics break. Advance by trigger, never by calendar.

Lusin lift assumption (×10) based on single pilot (Cyborg HR / Peter Kelly, April 2026); validate across 10+ deals before locking as benchmark. Mid-market integration cycle 60-120d is sensitive to founder availability during Phase 0.
